It is important to stay safe on roads and you have to be aware of everything

there is a lot of reckless drivers out there and it is important to stay alert

below is a few tips to stay safe while driving

Make sure everyone wears seatbelts

By wearing a seatbelt it will keep you secure inside the car

It will make sure you dont get ejected from your vehicle, you could be thrown out and injure yourself

Driving the speed limit

If you are speeding then you have less time to react

It is also harder to control the vehicle and your braking distance increases

Keep your distance

Every driver should know to keep a two second gap between you and the car in front

When in rain you should keep a four second gap because your braking distance would increases
