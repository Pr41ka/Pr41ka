Motorways can be very dangerous especially because because of the high speed

Below are a few tips for how to stay safe on the motorway

Always stay in the left lane

This is important because the right or middle lane is for overtaking

When you overtake a person and fail to move back to the left lane you cause people to undertake you which is illegal

Joining the motorway

You should adjust your speed on the slip road to match that of the cars in the left-hand lane of the main carriageway
