A theory test consists of two sections a hazard perception test and a theory test

A hazard perception is 14 video clips which are one minute long and there would be 15 hazards to find

Below are a few tips on passing first time

Take a few driving lessons before hand

By taking a few driving lessons you will get knowledge of road signs and basic road rules

Your driving instructor would usually explain basic hazards on the roads and this would benefit you as this would be covered in the test

Buy the theory test app

This app would benefit you a lot because it is only 5 pounds but this covers everything you need

This app also has mock videos for the hazard perception which is also part of the theory test

Revise the hazard perception tests

There are 14 clips that are selected randomly but it is important to revise all the videos becase you dont know which video you will get

It is also important for you not to click in a pattern or spam click as this will make it seem like you are cheating
